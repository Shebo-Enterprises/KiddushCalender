What is KiddushCalendar?

KiddushCalendar is an intuitive and powerful software designed specifically for synagogues to streamline the entire kiddush sponsorship and scheduling process. From collecting donations to publishing clear calendars, we've got you covered.

Key Features

Effortless Form Creation

Easily create customizable online forms for kiddush sponsorships, including options for dedications, dates, and donation amounts. Say goodbye to manual data entry!

Integrated Sponsorship Tracking

Keep a clear, organized record of all kiddush sponsorships, including sponsor details, and dedication information, all in one central location.

Dynamic Kiddush Calendars

Generate and publish beautiful, easy-to-read kiddush calendars directly from your collected data. Your congregants will always know what's happening and when.

Automated Notifications

Set up automatic email reminders for sponsors and administrators, ensuring no detail is missed.

Easy Integration

User-Friendly Interface
